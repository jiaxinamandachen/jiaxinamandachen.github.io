01 / About

London-based, internationally minded

I bring distinctive stories from first draft to final frame.

Independent producer and screenwriter with an MA in International Film Business from the London Film School. Across 6+ years and 14+ short films, I’ve led crews of 30+ from first idea to final delivery.

My practice spans project development, production management and culturally driven storytelling. I’m drawn to stories with a strong point of view—especially those moving between identities, genres and worlds.
